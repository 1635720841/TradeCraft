Are Clear Aligners Right for You
You may be a good candidate if you have mild to moderate crowding, small gaps, minor shifting, healthy gums, and can wear trays most of the day. This option can work well if you want a discreet treatment that fits around meals, work, and daily routines.

Traditional braces may work better for severe crowding, complex bite issues, untreated gum disease, or teeth that need stronger control. A dental exam confirms which option is safest for your mouth.

Clear Aligners vs Invisalign in SoHo
Clear aligners are removable trays that gradually move teeth, while Invisalign is a well-known brand in that treatment category. Unlike braces with metal brackets, the best choice depends on your bite, tooth-movement needs, your provider’s plan, and your daily habits.

How Treatment Works
Treatment works by moving teeth in small planned stages with a series of custom trays. You start with a consultation, digital scan, bite evaluation, and treatment plan, then wear each tray as directed before moving on to the next. Progress checks confirm that each tray fits well, your teeth are tracking, and your bite remains comfortable.

What to Know Before Starting
Your dentist should check for cavities, gum inflammation, bite stress, and tooth health before treatment.
